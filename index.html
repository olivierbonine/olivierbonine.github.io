Core product design rules

A compact set of principles to keep the library grounded in good product thinking.

01

Start with the problem

Understand the user's need before exploring a solution.

02

Reduce friction

Make the next step obvious and remove unnecessary complexity.

03

Design consistently

Reuse patterns and language so the experience feels familiar.

04

Validate early

Prototype quickly and test ideas before investing too much time.

05

Design for accessibility

Build for everyone, not only for the easiest use case.

06

Measure outcomes

Focus on the effect on users and the business, not just shipping features.
